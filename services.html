Specialized Services

In addition to our core departments, we offer specialized services to address specific healthcare needs.

Genetic Counseling

Our genetic counseling services help individuals and families understand and adapt to the medical, psychological, and familial implications of genetic contributions to disease.

Telemedicine

Access our healthcare professionals from the comfort of your home through our secure telemedicine platform, available for consultations and follow-ups.

Emergency Services

Our 24/7 emergency department is equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies with a team of specialized emergency medicine physicians and nurses.

Our home healthcare services bring medical care to your doorstep, including nursing care, physical therapy, and medical social services.
